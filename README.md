Shopping Record Notebook - Shopping Management Web App
购物记录小账本 - 购物管理网页应用
A simple and easy-to-use shopping record app that helps you manage daily purchases, track product usage status, and provides expiry and warranty reminders.
一个简单易用的购物记录应用，帮助你管理日常购物、跟踪商品使用状态，并提供到期提醒、质保期提醒等功能。
Key Features:
功能特点：
Product Category Management: Supports categories like Food, Electronics, Beauty, Daily Essentials, Clothing, and more.
Reminder System: Provides expiry and warranty period reminders for products with expiry or warranty dates, ensuring you don't miss important dates.
Daily Cost Calculation: Automatically calculates daily cost for durable goods (like electronics), helping you understand consumption efficiency.
Automatic Usage Days Calculation: Simply enter the purchase date, and the app automatically calculates the days the product has been used.
Image Upload: Supports uploading product images for easier visual management.
Local Storage: All records are stored locally in your browser, no need for external servers.
Simple and Intuitive Interface: Clean design and smooth functionality, perfect for everyday use.
商品分类管理：支持食品、电子产品、美妆、日用品、服装等分类。
提醒系统：对包含保质期或质保期的商品提供到期提醒，确保不会错过重要日期。
日均成本计算：对于耐用品（如电子产品）自动计算日均成本，帮助你了解消费效益。
自动计算使用天数：输入购买日期，系统自动计算商品已使用的天数。
上传商品图片：支持上传商品图片，便于视觉化管理。
本地存储：记录自动保存在本地，数据无需依赖外部服务器。
简洁易用的界面：设计简洁、直观，操作流畅，适合日常使用。
Use Cases:
使用场景：
Manage household shopping and consumption records, ensuring food items do not expire.
Track durable goods (like electronics and clothing) and calculate daily costs.
Quickly view product expiry dates and avoid missing warranty periods.
管理家庭日常购物、消费记录，确保食品等易腐商品不过期。
记录电子产品和服装等耐用品的使用情况，计算日均成本。
方便快捷地查看商品到期日期，避免错过质保期。

前端：HTML, CSS, JavaScript (Vanilla)
